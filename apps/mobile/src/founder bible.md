# Royal Mix Global – Founder Bible (v1.0)

## 1. What Royal Mix Global Is
Royal Mix Global is not an app.
It is not a product.
It is not a website.

Royal Mix Global is an **AI-powered Economic Operating System** that connects:
- Physical production
- Human agent networks
- Real market data
- Digital platforms
- Scalable ecosystems

It is built to start from the street and scale to governments and global companies.

---

## 2. Core System Loop (Economic Engine)

RAW MATERIALS  
(Soya, Cocoa, Local Inputs)  
↓  
PRODUCTION ENGINE  
(Royal Mix Choco & future products)  
↓  
FIELD OPERATIONS  
(Agents, Zones, Streets)  
↓  
DATA & FEEDBACK ENGINE  
(Sales, Demand, Taste, Logs)  
↓  
ROYAL MIX GLOBAL PLATFORM  
(AI, Dashboards, Products)  
↓  
SCALE & REPLICATION  
(Shops → Supermarkets → Regions → Countries)

This loop is permanent and self-improving.

- Real consumption data

- AI trained on real markets
- Multi-product ecosystem logic

Royal Mix Global competes at **system level**, not product level.
