This step ensures Royal Mix Global achieves worldwide visibility, trust, and growth through a fully integrated marketing and agent-driven scaling system.

---

## 17.1 Global Marketing Strategy

### Purpose
- Expand brand recognition globally
- Align all campaigns with Royal Mix Global values and systems
- Ensure all messaging supports AI-driven decision-making and self-evolving ecosystem

### Channels
- Social media (Facebook, TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn)
- Video platforms (YouTube, PiTube)
- Search & Ads (Google Ads, Pi Network Ads)
- Influencer partnerships (local + international)
- PR & media coverage

### Metrics
- Engagement (likes, shares, comments)
- Reach & impressions
- Conversion (downloads, agent signups, purchases)
- Retention & repeat usage

## 17.2 Agent Growth Engine

### Objectives
- Recruit the first 1,000 agents globally
- Ensure agents are onboarded, trained, and connected to Pi and Royal Mix Global platforms
- Track performance with KPIs for reward and incentive distribution

### Features
- Automated onboarding via mobile & web app
- Verification: ID, photo, phone, address
- Dashboard: stock, sales, customer data, reports
- Performance tracking: daily, weekly, monthly
- Commission & bonus management: 2–5 $ per task, cumulative monthly rewards

### Outcome
- Agents act as brand ambassadors
- Market intelligence captured automatically
- Incentives encourage loyalty & rapid expansion
