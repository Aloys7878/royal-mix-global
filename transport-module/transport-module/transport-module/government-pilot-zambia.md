# Zambia Government Pilot Proposal

## Objective
Demonstrate Royal Mix Global transport & delivery ecosystem for government/NGO operations.

## Pilot Scope
- 200 drivers, 100 road agents
- Lusaka initial rollout
- Passenger transport + government deliveries
- Performance metrics: punctuality, completion rate, satisfaction

## Integration
- Mobile app for drivers & agents
- Digital dashboard for government monitoring
- Secure payments & reporting

## KPIs
- Trips completed/day
- Deliveries executed
- Agent performance score
- Customer satisfaction
- Revenue per trip/order

## Outcome
- Show efficiency vs current providers (e.g., Yango)
- Provide scalable model for national rollout
- Collect real-time data for AI optimization
