# Royal Mix Global – Valuation & Exit Scenarios

## 1. WHY ROYAL MIX GLOBAL IS HIGH-VALUE

Royal Mix Global is:
- Infrastructure, not an app
- Multi-country ready
- Multi-sector (food, data, agents, government)
- AI-driven
- Network-effect based

Comparable to:
- Stripe (infrastructure)
- Uber (agent network)
- Palantir (government data)
- Alibaba (ecosystem)
