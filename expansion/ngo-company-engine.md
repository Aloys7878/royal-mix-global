# NGO & Company Integration Engine

## Objective
Allow Royal Mix Global to partner with NGOs and companies to expand reach, funding, and support.

## Key Features

- Partner onboarding system
- Project tracking & reporting
- Co-branded campaigns
- Real-time impact measurement

## Agent Role
- Collect data for NGO programs
- Support distribution of products & services
- Coordinate with local company partners

## Metrics & KPIs
- Total partner collaborations
- Products distributed via partners
- Revenue & impact from corporate/NGO programs
