# Data Intelligence Dashboard

## Objective
Provide a central hub for real-time monitoring and decision-making across agents, customers, and revenue streams.

## Key Features
- Visual KPIs (agents, sales, customers)
- Regional and global performance mapping
- Alerts for low stock, underperforming zones
- Forecasting & predictive analytics

## Metrics & KPIs
- Active agent engagement
- Sales per region & product
- Customer growth rate
- Predicted revenue trends
