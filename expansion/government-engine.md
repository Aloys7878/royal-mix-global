# Government Revenue Engine

## Objective
Enable Royal Mix Global to collaborate with government bodies for sustainable revenue streams.

## Key Features

- Tax-compliant invoicing
- Government supply contracts
- Subsidy & incentive programs
- Local regulatory compliance dashboards

## Agent Role
- Collect government product demand
- Report distribution metrics
- Ensure transparency via automated system

## Metrics & KPIs
- Government contract value per region
- Number of compliant deliveries
- Agent performance for government projects
- Real-time analytics for policy optimization
