# Agent Sales Data & Inventory

## Objective
Enable real-time tracking of agent product sales, stock, and customer metrics.

## Key Features
- Daily/weekly/monthly sales logging
- Stock management and restock alerts
- Customer acquisition & retention tracking
- Automated reports (per agent, region, product)

## Metrics & KPIs
- Products sold per agent / day
- Total active customers per agent
- Inventory turnover rate
- Revenue contribution per agent
