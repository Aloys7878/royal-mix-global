
# Global Expansion Metrics

## Core KPIs
- Number of active agents
- Daily transactions
- Verified products
- Data accuracy score
- Stakeholder adoption rate

## Expansion Triggers
Country moves to next level when:
- 1,000+ active agents
- Consistent daily data flow
- At least 1 government or NGO integration
- Profitable unit economics

## Risk Controls
- Slow expansion over fast expansion
- System stability > marketing growth
- Data quality > user quantity
