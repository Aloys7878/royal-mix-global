# Royal Mix Global – Monetization & Revenue Engine

Royal Mix Global is not a single-revenue app.
It is a multi-layer monetization ecosystem serving governments, NGOs,
companies, agents, and citizens.

---

## 1. GOVERNMENT REVENUE STREAMS

### 1.1 Digital Public Projects Licensing
Governments pay annual or project-based fees to host:
- Employment programs
- Food security projects
- Agent-based distribution systems
- Data dashboards (real-time)

Revenue model:
- Setup fee (USD 10,000 – 250,000 per project)
- Annual platform license
- Optional AI analytics add-on

---

### 1.2 National Agent Network Infrastructure
Royal Mix Global provides:
- Agent registration system
- ID + verification
- Reporting & monitoring
- Anti-fraud AI

Revenue:
- Per-agent yearly license
- Per-region deployment fee

---

## 2. NGO & INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS

### 2.1 Impact-as-a-Service
NGOs use Royal Mix Global to:
- Distribute aid
- Track beneficiaries
- Prevent duplication & corruption

Revenue:
- Monthly subscription
- Per-beneficiary tracking fee

---

### 2.2 Verified Impact Data Sales
Aggregated anonymized data:
- Poverty trends
- Food access
- Employment activity

Sold to:
- Donors
- Research institutions
- Development banks

## 3. COMPANIES & ENTERPRISES
