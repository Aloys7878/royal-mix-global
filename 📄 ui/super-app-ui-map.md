## Objective
Map for all screens, flows, modules in Super App

1. Login / Registration
2. Dashboard
3. Agent Management
4. Product Catalogue
5. Orders & Deliveries
6. Payments & Wallet
7. AI Recommendations
8. Reports & Analytics

- Payment processing → automated payouts → bonus calculations → trust scores

## Outcome
- Seamless navigation
- All modules integrated
- AI-powered, automated, global-scale
