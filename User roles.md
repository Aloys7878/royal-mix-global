## User Roles

- Customer
  - Browse products
  - Purchase products
  - Accumulate loyalty bonuses

- Shop Owner
  - Manage stock
  - Sell Royal Mix Choco products
  - Earn loyalty & volume bonuses

- Agent
  - Register customers & shops
  - Distribute products
  - Track commissions

- Distributor
  - Supply shops & agents
  - Monitor volumes

- Admin (Royal Mix Global)
  - Full system access
  - Analytics & governance
  - Product & pricing control
