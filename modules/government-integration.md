# Government Integration Module

## Goal
Guhuza Royal Mix Global na:
- Governments
- Ministries
- Cities & Municipalities
- Public Services

## Supported Use Cases
- Population data insights (non-sensitive)
- Informal market formalization
- SME registration support
- Tax transparency (optional)
- Social programs distribution

## Government Benefits
- Real-time economic visibility
- Reduced corruption
- Faster citizen services
- Policy impact measurement

## Integration Methods
- APIs
- Dashboards
- Secure data sharing
- Read-only or write access (controlled)

## Trust Principle
Government sees **aggregated intelligence**, not private user data.

## Strategic Value
Royal Mix Global becomes a **national digital infrastructure partner**.
