# Royal Mix Global – AI Core Engine

## Purpose
AI Core ni ubwonko bwa Royal Mix Global. Ni bwo bufata data zose, bukazisuzuma, bukamenya ivyo abantu, agents, governments, companies n’amasoko bakeneye mu kanya.

## Core Capabilities
- Real-time decision making
- Predictive demand & supply
- Automatic recommendations
- Self-learning (feedback loops)
- Multilingual & local-context AI

## AI Layers
1. User Behavior AI
2. Agent Performance AI
3. Market Prediction AI
4. Government Policy AI
5. Risk & Fraud Detection AI

## Why it’s unique
- Works offline → syncs online
- Learns from Africa-first realities
- Not static → evolves daily

## Outputs
- Actions (not only insights)
- Auto-executed workflows
- Alerts, tasks, approvals

## Result
Royal Mix Global becomes a **living system**, not an app.
