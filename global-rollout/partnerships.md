# Global Partnerships Strategy

## Purpose
- Establish trust and collaboration with governments, NGOs, and private enterprises
- Ensure regulatory compliance in all target countries
- Expand Royal Mix Global presence quickly and sustainably

## Partnership Categories
1. **Government & Municipal Agencies**
   - Secure licensing & approvals
   - Public-private partnerships for distribution
   - Policy alignment & support

2. **NGOs & Social Organizations**
   - Collaboration for social projects
   - Community engagement programs
   - Data & insight sharing

3. **Corporate & Enterprise**
   - Distribution contracts
   - Co-marketing opportunities
   - Sponsorship & investments

## Key Actions
- Legal & compliance check per country
- Establish local representative offices
- Negotiate pilot programs
- Provide technical integration for partners
- Continuous monitoring of KPIs & deliverables
