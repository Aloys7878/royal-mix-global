# Global AI Marketplace Overview

## Purpose
- Create a marketplace for AI-powered services and tools
- Allow agents, customers, and partners to access monetized AI services
- Facilitate global expansion with automated supply & demand matching

## Key Features
- AI tools catalog (Royal Mix AI, analytics, prediction engines)
- Agent & partner onboarding
- Automated service discovery
- Subscription & pay-per-use models
- Ratings & reviews for trust and quality
