# Incentive & Revenue Engine

## Agent Earnings Model

### Option A – Commission Based
- 2–5% per transaction
- Daily, weekly, monthly tracking
- Performance-based scaling

### Option B – Hybrid Model
- Base reward after requirements met
- Plus monthly commissions
- Reduces fraud and churn

### Bonuses
- Daily consistency bonus
- Weekly top-performer bonus
- Zone achievement bonus
- Referral bonus
- Data quality bonus

## Platform Revenue Streams
- Transaction fees
- Subscriptions (companies, NGOs)
- Data services (aggregated, anonymized)
- Sponsored projects
- AI tools & automation
- Marketplace commissions
