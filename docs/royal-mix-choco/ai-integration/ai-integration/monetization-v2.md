# Monetization Engine v2

## Objectives
- Enable multiple revenue streams
- Automate payouts for agents, drivers, and partners
- Connect with AI insights for dynamic pricing

## Revenue Streams
- Product sales (Royal Mix Choco & other products)
- Transport fees
- Premium subscriptions (VIP agents/customers)
- Marketplace commissions
- Government / NGO contracts
- Advertising & sponsored content

## Dynamic Pricing
- AI-driven demand-based pricing
- Surge pricing for high-demand zones
- Discounts and promotions automatically applied

## Payment Flows
- Integrated wallets (Cash, Mobile Money, Pi Network)
- Automated payouts based on performance
- Bonus & incentive distribution
- Daily/weekly/monthly reporting

## KPIs
- Revenue per module / agent / driver
- Daily active users & orders
- Monetization rate per customer
- ROI per marketing & expansion activity
